I have recently moved to the International Computer Science Institute (ICSI). Previously, I was an assistant professor at NYU Abu Dhabi. My work focuses on human-computer interaction and systems building applied to societal problems. Much of my previous work is in the area of information and communication technologies for development (ICTD). I've recently started focusing more on sustainability, e.g. Edible Fire Breaks to help mitigate the California wildfires.
